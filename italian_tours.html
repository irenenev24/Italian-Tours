PLACES TO VISIT IN ITALY

Rome

A heady mix of haunting ruins, awe-inspiring art and vibrant street life, Italy’s hot-blooded capital is one of the world’s most romantic and charismatic cities.

Abruzzo

Featuring unspoilt beaches, natural lakes, beautiful landscapes and authentic villages, Abruzzo has everything needed to help you relax in a tranquil setting.

Sicily

Eternal crossroads of the Mediterranean, the gorgeous island of Sicily continues to seduce travellers with its dazzling diversity of landscapes and cultural treasures.

TOURIST ATTRACTIONS IN ITALY

Catacombs, Rome

The Catacombs of Rome are former underground burial grounds that date from the second to the fifth century and were principally used by Christians and Jews.

Lake Scanno, Abruzzo

Lake Scanno is the largest natural lake in Abruzzo. Here you will find boats to hire, beautiful scenery and water that has won a blue flag making it perfect for swimming.

San Vito Lo Capo, Sicily

Here, you will find a stunning stretch of white sandy beach at the foot of Monte Monaco which has made San Vito Lo Capo so famous in Italy and beyond. Surrounded by quaint villages, locals view the surroundings from sun soaked balconies.
