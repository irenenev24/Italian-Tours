Abruzzo

Boasting mountains, beaches and a nature reserve that is second to none, Abruzzo is the jewel in Italy's crown. Here you will find the largest natural lake in Italy, Lake Scanno surround by untouch landscapes and villages that have been sheltered from the march of time. For a true Italian experience, Abruzzo is the number one choice.
